Cookie Policy

Information about cookies used on business-english.club

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and, in some cases, help website owners understand how visitors use the site.

2. How this website uses cookies

The website business-english.club may use cookies to improve functionality and user experience.

At the moment, the website primarily uses necessary cookies. If additional cookies are introduced in the future, users will be informed and asked for consent where required by law.

3. Types of cookies that may be used

Necessary cookies
These cookies are essential for the operation of the website and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies
These cookies help understand how visitors interact with the website.

Preference cookies
These cookies remember user settings and preferences.

4. Managing cookie preferences

Users may control cookie preferences through the cookie banner displayed on the website. Your browser settings may also allow you to block or delete cookies.

5. Contact

If you have questions about the use of cookies on this website, please contact admin@business-english.club.
